About Nevear Games

Born from late-night conversations about the nature of fear, Nevear Games is an independent studio dedicated to exploring the darkest corners of human experience. We believe horror transcends jump scares—it's about the slow unraveling of what we thought we understood about ourselves and the world around us.

Each project begins with a question: "What keeps you awake at 3 AM?" From these whispers in the dark, we craft experiences that don't just frighten, but disturb, unsettle, and ultimately reveal something true about the human condition.
